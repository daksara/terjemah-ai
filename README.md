### ✨ What makes it different

Most translators just convert words. **Terjemah.AI** goes further — after translating, it also reads the **tone** of your text and responds accordingly:

- 😊 Positive sentence → gets a warm, encouraging response
- 😔 Negative sentence → gets an empathetic response  
- ❓ Question → gets a helpful conversational reply

---

### 🚀 How to Use

**Step 1** — Get a free Groq API Key at [console.groq.com](https://console.groq.com)

**Step 2** — Open the app and click **⚙️ API Key**, paste your key

**Step 3** — Type or paste your English text

**Step 4** — Click **Terjemahkan →** (or press `Ctrl+Enter`)

**Step 5** — Get your Indonesian translation + contextual response
